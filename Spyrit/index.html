Features

Notifications

You can add events to the in-app calender and set the app to remind you about upcoming events.

Locations

You can use Google Maps within the app to find the events and get directions.

Reviews

You and other users can comment and rate events that you have visited in the app.

Chat

You can chat with other users with the in-app messaging system.

Purchases

The app lets you purchase admission to events such as movies, when possible.

Invite

You and your friends can invite each other to events through the app.
